Scannability & Compliance Basics

  • UPC-A: 12 digits (check digit included). Retail use in US/Canada.
  • EAN-13: 13 digits (global retail). EAN-8 uses 8 digits for small packages.
  • Quiet Zone: Keep clear margins (left/right) so scanners can detect start/stop patterns.
  • Contrast: Prefer dark bars on a light background (e.g., black on white) for best results.
  • Print: Use SVG for sharp output on labels/packaging; avoid low-DPI rasterization.

Tip: If a barcode fails to show, the input length/charset may not match the selected standard (e.g., EAN/UPC require strict digit counts).
