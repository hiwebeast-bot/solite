About the Hashtag Separator Tool

The Solite Hashtag Separator is a free, instant tool that cleans up blocks of hashtags. Simply paste your hashtags, and it automatically adds spaces between them, making them perfectly formatted and readable for platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter).

How It Works

  1. Paste Hashtags: Copy your block of hashtags (e.g., from a generator or document) and paste it into the input box on the left.
  2. Instant Formatting: The tool automatically processes the text, adding a space before each hashtag (`#`).
  3. Copy Result: Click the "Copy" button to grab the clean, space-separated hashtags, ready to be pasted into your social media posts.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Improved Readability: Space-separated hashtags are easier for you and your audience to read.
  • Saves Time: No more manually adding spaces between dozens of hashtags.
  • 100% Private: All formatting happens in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
  • Free and Unlimited: Use it as much as you need, completely free.
