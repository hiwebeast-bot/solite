At Solite, your privacy is the core principle of our architecture. Our commitment is to provide powerful, free tools without compromising your data. This policy outlines how we protect your privacy by fundamentally avoiding data collection.

1. Our Core Principle: Client-Side Processing

2. Information We Don't Collect

To be perfectly clear, we do not require user accounts and therefore do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as:

Please note that we do not share any of the data you process with our tools with these advertisers.

5. Data Security

While your data remains on your device, we take measures to secure our website itself. All traffic to and from solite.in is encrypted using HTTPS, protecting the integrity of the web pages and scripts loaded into your browser.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Regulations like the GDPR and CCPA grant users rights over their personal data, such as the right to access, correct, and delete it. Since Solite does not collect or store your personal data, you inherently have the highest level of control: your data never leaves your possession. If you use a tool that saves information in your browser's local storage, you can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our services or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any significant changes will be clearly communicated. However, our core commitment to being a client-side, privacy-first service is fundamental to our mission and will not change.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please do not hesitate to contact us.
