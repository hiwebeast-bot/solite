Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 19, 2025

At Solite, your privacy is not just a policy; it's the core principle of our architecture. We are committed to protecting your data by not collecting it in the first place.

1. No Data Collection

Solite operates entirely within your web browser. When you use our tools—whether it's the Text Engine, Image Editor, Visual Pad, or Certificate Maker—all data, files, and content you provide are processed on your own device (client-side). We do not have servers that receive, store, or process your personal data or the content you work with.

2. No User Accounts

We do not require you to sign up, log in, or create an account to use our services. This ensures that we do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or any other identifying details.

3. Local Storage

Some of our tools, like the Visual Pad, may use your browser's local storage to save your work automatically. This data is stored only on your computer and is not transmitted to us. You have full control over this data and can clear it at any time through your browser's settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services for tracking, analytics, or advertising that would compromise your privacy. Our goal is to provide a clean, focused, and private experience.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy model, we will update this policy accordingly. However, our commitment to being a client-side, privacy-first service is fundamental to our mission.
