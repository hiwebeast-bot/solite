Secure Online Text Encryption & Decryption Tool

The Solite Text Encrypter is a free, secure tool to password-protect your text messages using the powerful AES-GCM encryption standard. Everything happens directly in your browser, ensuring your data and password are never sent to a server. It's the perfect way to share sensitive information privately.

Why Use This Tool?

  • 100% Private: All encryption and decryption are performed client-side. Your data never leaves your device.
  • Strong Encryption: Uses AES-GCM, a modern, authenticated encryption algorithm trusted worldwide.
  • Simple to Use: Just enter your text, a password, and click a button. No complex setup required.
  • Free & Accessible: No sign-up, no limits. A powerful security tool available to everyone.

How It Works

  1. To Encrypt: Enter your plain text and a strong password, then click "Encrypt". Copy the resulting encrypted string.
  2. To Decrypt: Paste the encrypted string, enter the exact same password, and click "Decrypt". Your original text will appear.
