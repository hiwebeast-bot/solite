What is 'bad software' in the context of free online tools?

Bad software refers to 'free' online tools that have a poor user experience, such as being slow, covered in ads, or requiring excessive personal information. More importantly, it includes tools that compromise user privacy by uploading and storing sensitive data on third-party servers without clear consent.
