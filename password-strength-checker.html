    The Solite Password Strength Checker provides instant, actionable feedback on your password's security. It runs 100% locally in your browser using the industry-standard zxcvbn library. Your password is never sent to a server, ensuring it remains completely private.

    Why This Tool is Secure & Private

    • No Data Transmission: All analysis happens on your device. Nothing is sent over the network.
    • Industry-Standard Library: Uses Dropbox's open-source zxcvbn library, trusted by millions.
    • Actionable Feedback: Provides real-world "time to crack" estimates, not just arbitrary "medium" or "strong" labels.
    • Requirement Checklist: Instantly validates common requirements (length, case, numbers, symbols).

    How to Use

    1. Type your password into the input field.
    2. Instantly see the requirement checklist (length, case, etc.) update.
    3. Review the strength bar (from "Very Weak" to "Very Strong").
    4. Check the "Time to crack" estimate for a real-world metric.
    5. Read the "Suggestions" for specific ways to improve your password.
