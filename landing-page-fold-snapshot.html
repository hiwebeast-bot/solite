Why "The Fold" Matters

The content above the fold determines whether a user stays or leaves. According to Nielsen Norman Group, users spend 57% of their page-viewing time above the fold. This tool helps you ensure your primary call-to-action (CTA), value proposition, and key visuals are immediately visible.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Enter your website URL and click the arrow button.
  2. Instantly view the initial screen for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewports.
  3. Analyze if your key message and CTA are visible without scrolling.
  4. Use the device dropdown to test specific or custom screen sizes.
  5. Drag the preview area horizontally to see all device snapshots at once.
