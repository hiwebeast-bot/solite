About the Emoji Counter Tool

The Solite Emoji Counter is a free, privacy-focused online utility designed for social media managers, content creators, and anyone who needs to quickly analyze the emojis in their text. Unlike other tools, our counter runs entirely in your browser, which means your text is never uploaded to a server. It's 100% private, secure, and incredibly fast.

Key Features:

  • Total & Unique Emoji Count: Instantly see how many emojis are in your text and how many of them are unique.
  • Emoji Frequency List: Get a detailed, sorted list of every emoji used and how many times it appears.
  • Emoji Density: Understand the ratio of emojis to words in your text, a useful metric for gauging the tone and visual appeal of your content.
  • One-Click Copy: Easily copy the summary statistics or individual emojis from the frequency list to use in your posts, reports, or analytics.
  • Privacy by Design: All processing is done client-side. Your data never leaves your device.
