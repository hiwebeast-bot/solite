The Solite Delivery Invoice Generator is a versatile tool for e-commerce and businesses that ship goods. Create detailed delivery invoices or switch to a price-free Packing Slip with a single click. Add SKUs, tracking numbers, and shipping methods. Everything is processed 100% inside your browser for complete privacy.

Logistics Features for Free

  • Dual Mode: Toggle prices on/off to instantly switch between a Delivery Invoice (with costs) and a Packing Slip (item checklist only).
  • Essential Shipping Info: Include fields for Shipping Method and Tracking Number on your documents.
  • Product SKU Support: Add a unique SKU for each line item, essential for accurate inventory management and order fulfillment.
  • Professional Branding: Upload your company logo for branded shipping documents.
  • Auto-Calculating Engine: When in invoice mode, automatically handles subtotal, taxes, discounts, and totals.
  • Flexible Export: Save as a professional PDF, PNG, or JPG, ready to print or email.

How to Create a Packing Slip

  1. Fill in your shipping details and the customer's delivery address.
  2. Add line items, including their SKU and quantity.
  3. Uncheck the "Show Prices (Packing Slip Mode)" box in the options section. This will hide all financial data.
  4. Add any special handling instructions in the Shipping Notes box.
  5. Choose your export format (PDF is recommended for printing).
  6. Click Download to save your ready-to-print packing slip.
