The Solite CPC Budget Split Calculator is a free tool for marketers to instantly allocate a budget across multiple channels. Split your ad spend evenly or by custom ratios, and forecast key performance metrics like estimated clicks, conversions, and cost-per-acquisition (CPA). It's fully private, client-side, and requires no sign-up.

Why Use This Budget Splitter?

  • Instant Calculation: See budget splits in real-time as you adjust values.
  • Performance Forecasting: Enter average CPC and conversion rates to estimate clicks, conversions, and CPA for each channel.
  • Flexible Allocation: Choose between an even split or a proportional (ratio-based) split.
  • 100% Private: All calculations happen in your browser. No data is sent or saved anywhere.
  • Unlimited Channels: Add as many advertising channels as you need for your campaign.

How to Use the Budget Split Calculator

  1. Set Budget & Duration: Input your total budget and the campaign duration in days.
  2. Add Channels: Click "Add Channel" and name them (e.g., "Google Search," "Meta Ads").
  3. Choose Split Method:
    • Tick "Split Budget Evenly" for an equal distribution.
    • For a proportional split, leave it unchecked and enter a ratio for each channel (e.g., 3 for Google, 2 for Meta).
  4. (Optional) Forecast Performance: Enter the average CPC and expected conversion rate (%) for each channel to see estimated clicks, conversions, and CPA.
  5. View Results: The allocated budget and performance metrics for each channel appear instantly on the right.
